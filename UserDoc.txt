Installation Instructions:
1. Download the psumaps.apk file from https://github.com/saiswathi/psumaps/blob/master/bin/psumaps.apk to your computer.
2. Connect the android device to the computer. Be sure to enable USB debugging on the device. To do that go to Settings>Developer Option.
3. Copy the psumaps.apk file to any folder in your android device.
4. Open the psumaps.apk file in your android device and follow the instructions to install the app.

Usage Instructions:

Find the installed app in your android device and open the app. Ensure that your phone is connected to the internet.
